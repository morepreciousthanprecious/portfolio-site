Matthew Hedgepath, Woodworker

Matthew Hedgepath is a woodworker who focuses his craftmanship on traditional techniques, using hand tools rather than solely machinery, to build pieces of custom furniture.

In order to represent the influence of tradition in his craft, it was necessary to conduct research on craftsmens marks as well as typographic styles from the 19th and early 20th century. The aesthetics of furniture makers and brands of that era served as a guide for the final design.
