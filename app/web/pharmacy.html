Web Design, Logo, and Custom Icon for an Independent Pharmacy

This is a practice project where I redesigned the logo and website for an independently-owned pharmacy. The pharmacy, that has been in business for over 50 years in the same location, has kept its original sign at the storefront that features the company name in a geometric typeface in green. The modernized logotype took reference from the original type on the sign, and has been updated to the current standards of clean, minimal design.
